About Us

This watch ecommerce website was built on the Magento platform, an open source content management system for commerce.
Online shoppers like to see large, clear and detailed photos of the products they are browsing.
These users also want options if the current product does not meet their needs.
The product detail page improved merchandising, cross sell areas and features a 360 view of product.
A unique shopping feature is the way a user can see the watches on their own wrist with virtual reality.
